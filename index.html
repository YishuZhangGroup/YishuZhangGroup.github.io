About Our Research Group

Welcome to the Yishu Zhang Research Group

Our research group at Zhejiang University's College of Integrated Circuits focuses on cutting-edge technologies in semiconductor devices, microelectronics, and integrated circuit design. Led by Professor Yishu Zhang, we aim to address fundamental challenges in electronics and contribute to technological advancements.

Our work spans both theoretical and experimental approaches, with collaborations across disciplines and institutions worldwide. We are committed to training the next generation of researchers and innovators in the field of integrated circuits.

Research Areas

Semiconductor Devices

Investigation of novel semiconductor materials and device structures for next-generation electronics applications.

Neuromorphic Computing

Development of brain-inspired computing architectures using emerging electronic devices for AI applications.

Low-Power IC Design

Design and optimization of energy-efficient integrated circuits for IoT and mobile applications.
